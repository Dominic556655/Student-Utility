Refund Policy

Understanding refunds for Ichason services and digital tools

Last updated: 2026

⚠️ Please read this Refund Policy carefully before using our services.

1. Digital Services

Ichason provides digital educational tools such as GPA calculators, CGPA systems, study planners, and related academic services. Since these are instantly accessible digital services, we generally do not offer refunds once access is granted.

2. Subscription or Paid Features (If Applicable)

If any premium features or subscriptions are introduced in the future, refunds will only be considered under the following conditions:

  • Duplicate payment was made
  • Technical error prevented access to paid features
  • Service was not delivered as described

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Change of mind after using the service
  • User misunderstanding of features
  • Failure to read instructions or guidelines

4. Refund Request Process

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, contact our support team with proof of payment and details of the issue. All requests will be reviewed within a reasonable timeframe.

5. Contact

For refund-related inquiries, please contact us through the support page on the platform.
