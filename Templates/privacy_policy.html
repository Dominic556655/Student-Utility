Privacy Policy

How Ichason handles your data, security, and academic records

Last updated: 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you register or use our platform. This includes:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Academic data (GPA, CGPA, course records)
  • Any data you save on the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide GPA/CGPA calculation services
  • Store your academic history
  • Improve user experience
  • Maintain account security

3. Data Storage

All data is securely stored in our database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law.

5. User Control

  • View your saved data
  • Delete your records
  • Update your profile information

6. Cookies

We may use cookies to improve login sessions and user experience.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of major changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us through the platform support page.
